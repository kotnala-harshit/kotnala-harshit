## Featured Projects

### 📈 [Stock Market Manipulation Detection](https://github.com/kotnala-harshit/stock-manipulation-detection)
*UCC Final Research Project · Deloitte Mentored*

Led a 6-member team building an Isolation Forest anomaly detection pipeline across 467,799 records (Russell 2000, 2024). Shifted compliance use case from post-crash investigation to proactive pre-dump intervention.

- **F1-score 0.76** · Precision 0.75 · Recall 0.77 · Accuracy 0.99
- Identified **7,661 critical risk events** (1.6% of records) across ~2,000 small-cap equities
- 3-phase Manipulation Signature Curve via EMA-based signal logic
- 4-horizon Random Forest forecasting (7-day → 6-month)
- Power BI compliance dashboard surfacing top 50 critical events

`Python` `Scikit-learn` `Isolation Forest` `Random Forest` `Power BI` `Yahoo Finance API`

### 🏭 [Global Supply Chain Optimization](https://github.com/kotnala-harshit/supply-chain-optimization)
*Prescriptive Analytics · MSc Business Analytics, UCC*

MILP + Dijkstra routing + Monte Carlo simulation for a global luxury supply chain (4 suppliers, 3 factories, 2 warehouses, 6 global cities). CO₂-penalised objective minimising cost under uncertain demand.

- 9-scenario stochastic tree (3 supply × 3 demand levels)
- 1,000-iteration Monte Carlo varying supply, demand, and staff absenteeism
- Warehouse capacity (220K units) identified as the binding constraint
- Dijkstra routing: time-based for urgent cities, cost-based for standard

`Python` `PuLP` `NetworkX` `Monte Carlo` `MILP` `Dijkstra`

### 📊 [JPY/USD Exchange Rate Forecasting](https://github.com/kotnala-harshit/jpy-usd-forecasting)
*Business Forecasting · MSc Business Analytics, UCC*

PPP testing and ARIMA(2,1,0) forecasting of JPY/USD real exchange rate using 240 monthly observations (2000–2019) from FRED.

- Absolute and relative PPP rejected in the short run (consistent with Rogoff 1996)
- Engle-Granger cointegration confirmed — PPP holds as long-run anchor
- **ARIMA(2,1,0)** selected by AIC/BIC · MAPE **0.75%** over 12-month forecast horizon
- Structural break identified: 2008 GFC + Abenomics

`R` `ARIMA` `ADF/KPSS` `Engle-Granger` `Bai-Perron` `FRED`
